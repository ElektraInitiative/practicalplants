Grape


is a common name used for a number of distinct species.

  • Mahonia nervosa (Oregon Grape)
  • Mahonia repens (Creeping Oregon Grape)
  • Muscari botryoides (Italian Grape Hyacinth)
  • Muscari neglectum (Grape Hyacinth)

  • Vitis aestivalis (Summer Grape)
  • Vitis aestivalis argentifolia (Summer Grape)
