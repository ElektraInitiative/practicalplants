Hyacinth


is a common name used for a number of distinct species.

Plants with the common name Hyacinth

B

  • Bletilla striata (Hyacinth Orchid)
  • Brodiaea coronaria (Californian Hyacinth)

C

  • Camassia leichtlinii (Wild Hyacinth)

D

  • Dichelostemma pulchellum (Wild Hyacinth)

E

  • Eichhornia crassipes (Water Hyacinth)

H

  • Hyacinthus orientalis (Hyacinth)

L

  • Lablab purpureus (Hyacinth Bean)

M

  • Muscari botryoides (Italian Grape Hyacinth)

M cont.

  • Muscari comosum (Tassel Hyacinth)
  • Muscari neglectum (Grape Hyacinth)

T

  • Triteleia grandiflora (Wild Hyacinth)
  • Triteleia hyacintha (Hyacinth Brodiaea)
