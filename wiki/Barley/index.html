Barley


is a common name used for a number of distinct species.

Plants with the common name Barley

A

  • Astrebla pectinata (Barley Mitchell Grass)

H

  • Hordeum bulbosum (Bulbous Barley)
  • Hordeum distichon (Two-Rowed Barley)

H cont.

  • Hordeum hexastichon (Six-Row Barley)
  • Hordeum jubatum (Foxtail Barley)
  • Hordeum murinum (Mouse Barley)

H cont.

  • Hordeum trifurcatum (Egyptian Barley)
  • Hordeum vulgare (Barley)
  • Hordeum zeocriton (Sprat Barley)
