Windbreak

is a function performed by some plants which, by their size and form, are able to provide shelter from winds.


A-Z of plants which function as a Windbreak

A

  • Acer negundo (Box Elder)
  • Acer platanoides (Norway Maple)
  • Acer pseudoplatanus (Sycamore)
  • Acer saccharinum (Silver Maple)
  • Alnus cordata (Italian Alder)
  • Alnus glutinosa (Alder)
  • Alnus rubra (Red Alder)
  • Amelanchier alnifolia (Saskatoon)
  • Amorpha canescens (Lead Plant)
  • Amorpha fruticosa (False Indigo)
  • Arundo donax (Giant Reed)
  • Atriplex halimus (Saltbush Sea Orach)

B

  • Bumelia lanuginosa (Chittamwood)

C

  • Caragana arborescens (Siberian Pea Tree)
  • Caragana boisii
  • Caragana fruticosa
  • Casuarina cunninghamiana (River She-Oak)

C cont.

  • Cotoneaster conspicuus (Tibetan Cotoneaster)

  • Cryptomeria japonica (Japanese Cedar)
  • Cupressocyparis leylandii (Leyland Cypress)
  • Cupressus macrocarpa (Monterey Cypress)

E

  • Elaeagnus umbellata (Autumn Olive)
