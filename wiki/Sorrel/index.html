is a common name used for a number of distinct species.

Plants with the common name Sorrel

O

  • Oxalis acetosella (Wood Sorrel)
  • Oxalis articulata (Pink Sorrel)
  • Oxalis corniculata (Yellow Sorrel)
  • Oxalis exilis (Least Yellow Sorrel)
  • Oxalis montana (Mountain Wood Sorrel)

O cont.

  • Oxalis oregana (Redwood Sorrel)
  • Oxalis stricta (Yellow Wood Sorrel)
  • Oxalis violacea (Violet Wood Sorrel)
  • Oxydendrum arboreum (Sorrel Tree)
  • Oxyria digyna (Mountain Sorrel)

R

  • Rumex acetosa (Sorrel)
  • Rumex acetosella (Sheeps Sorrel)
  • Rumex arifolius (Maiden Sorrel)
  • Rumex scutatus (Buckler-Leaved Sorrel)
