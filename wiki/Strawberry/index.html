Strawberry


is a common name used for a number of distinct species.

Plants with the common name Strawberry

A

  • Arbutus andrachne (Grecian StrawberryTree)
  • Arbutus unedo (Strawberry Tree)

C

  • Chenopodium capitatum (Strawberry Blite)

D

  • Duchesnea indica (Mock Strawberry)

E

  • Euonymus americanus (Strawberry Bush)

F

  • Fragaria bracteata (Woodland Strawberry)
  • Fragaria californica (Californian Strawberry)
  • Fragaria chiloensis (Beach Strawberry)

F cont.

  • Fragaria iinumae (Strawberry)
  • Fragaria moschata (Hautbois Strawberry)
  • Fragaria nubicola (Indian Strawberry)
  • Fragaria ovalis (Rocky Mountain Strawberry)
  • Fragaria vesca (Wild Strawberry)
  • Fragaria vesca 'Semperflorens' (Alpine Strawberry)
  • Fragaria virginiana (Scarlet Strawberry)
  • Fragaria viridis (Green Strawberry)

F cont.

  • Fragaria x ananassa (Strawberry)

P

  • Physalis pruinosa (Strawberry Tomato)
  • Polymnia edulis (Yacon Strawberry)
  • Psidium cattleianum (Strawberry Guava)
  • Psidium cattleianum littorale (Yellow Strawberry Guava)

R

  • Rubus illecebrosus (Strawberry-Raspberry)

S

  • Saxifraga stolonifera (Strawberry Saxifrage)
