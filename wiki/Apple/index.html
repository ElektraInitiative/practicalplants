Apple


is a common name used for a number of distinct species.

Plants with the common name Apple

A

  • Amelanchier lamarckii (Apple Serviceberry)
  • Angophora cordifolia (Smooth-Barked Apple)
  • Angophora floribunda (Rough-Barked Apple)

B

  • Billardiera cymosa (Sweet Appleberry)
  • Billardiera longiflora (Appleberry)
  • Billardiera scandens (Common Appleberry)

D

  • Datura inoxia (Downy Thorn Apple)
  • Datura metel (Thorn Apple)
  • Datura stramonium (Thorn Apple)

M

  • Malus baccata mandschurica (Manchurian Apple)
  • Malus domestica (Apple)

M cont.

  • Malus prunifolia (Chinese Apple)
  • Malus prunifolia rinkii (Chinese Apple)
  • Malus pumila (Paradise Apple)
  • Malus pumila nervosa (Crab Apple)
  • Malus pumila paradisiaca (Paradise Apple)
  • Malus spectabilis (Chinese Flowering Apple)
  • Malus sylvestris (Crab Apple)
  • Malus x micromalus (Kaido Crab Apple)
  • Mentha x villosa alopecuroides (Apple Mint)
  • Myoporum debile (Winter Apple)

P

  • Pelargonium odoratissimum (Apple Geranium)

P cont.

  • Peraphyllum ramosissimum (Squaw Apple)
  • Planchonella australis (Black Apple)
  • Podophyllum hexandrum (Himalayan May Apple)

R

  • Rosa villosa (Apple Rose)

S

  • Salvia pomifera (Apple Sage)
  • Solanum aviculare (Kangaroo Apple)
  • Solanum laciniatum (Kangaroo Apple)
  • Solanum linearifolium (Mountain Kangaroo Apple)
  • Solanum vescum (Green Kangaroo Apple)
