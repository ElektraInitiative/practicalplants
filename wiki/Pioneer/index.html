Pioneer

is a function performed by a plant which is able to grow in conditions unfavorable to others and in so doing improve the environmental conditions to a degree that they are able to sustain life which could previously not colonise that environment.

A-Z of plants which function as a Pioneer

  • Acer pseudoplatanus (Sycamore)
  • Acer rubrum (Red Maple)
  • Alnus cordata (Italian Alder)
  • Alnus glutinosa (Alder)
  • Alnus rubra (Red Alder)

  • Alnus sinuata (Sitka Alder)

  • Betula papyrifera (Paper Birch)
